Academic Overview

PM SHRI Govt. Sr. Sec. School Ajeetpura provides a diverse and holistic academic experience. We focus on excellence in both Arts and Science streams, fostering creativity, critical thinking, and discipline in our students.

Senior Secondary Curriculum

Offering Science and Arts streams, including core subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, Political Science, and History, along with optional subjects like Computer Science.

Modern Facilities

Fully equipped laboratories, computer rooms, and a well-stocked library with 2000+ books, fostering an environment conducive to learning and exploration.

Examination System

Continuous evaluation with regular mid-term assessments and final exams, adhering to the guidelines set by the Rajasthan Board of Secondary Education (RBSE).

Academic Achievements

Consistent performance in board exams, with many students achieving scholarships and merit-based awards at district and state levels.

Subject Groups

Science Stream

Offering subjects like Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Mathematics, and English, along with an emphasis on practical work and experimentation in laboratories.

Arts Stream

Subjects include Political Science, History, Geography, Hindi, and English. Students in this stream are encouraged to develop analytical and creative skills.

Co-Curricular Activities

Sports & Physical Education

We offer a wide variety of sports activities, including cricket, volleyball, and athletics, to encourage physical fitness and team spirit among students.

Cultural Activities

Annual cultural programs that celebrate student talents in music, dance, drama, and other forms of creative expression, providing a well-rounded education.
